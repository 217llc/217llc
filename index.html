Our Approach

We are always buying.

217 Holdings is a Quincy, Illinois-based real estate investment company. We focus on the middle American market — properties and opportunities that institutional money overlooks and local operators are positioned to execute on.

We hold assets for long-term cash flow, improve neighborhoods one property at a time, and build relationships that outlast any single transaction. Bring us your problem properties. We will bring you an offer.

The principal is a licensed managing broker in the state of Illinois with hands-on experience across acquisition, development, and asset management.
